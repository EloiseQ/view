4Conclusion

  • 8 Core Scale campaigns absorb ~96% of SBD budget (~$595K) and generated 86% of historical revenue. These are the primary spend vehicles — mature hero products (MB Trio, Happy Burn, Berberine, Beauty Burn). Accept ROI compression to 1.5–2.0× for volume.
  • 9 Efficient Expansion campaigns contribute 10% of revenue at higher efficiency. They receive moderate budget and slightly tighter ROI guardrails (2.0–2.5× during SBD) — scale selectively, not aggressively.
  • 3 Secondary Hero Launch campaigns funded by Launch Test Budget ($18K extra, under GMV Max marketing). Super Green Single, Super Green + Berberine, Metabolic Quatra. Launch test case; SBD = optimal traffic window. No max delivery.
  • 19 Controlled Testing campaigns account for 4% of revenue. They should not absorb meaningful SBD budget; keep ROI floors at 2.5–3.5× and avoid max delivery.
  • Max delivery is reserved for proven Core Scale campaigns during campaign week only. Efficient Expansion may use it selectively. Secondary Hero Launch and Testing campaigns never use it.
  • All daily cost ranges are derived from historical phase behavior × bucket multipliers, not fabricated precision. They should be treated as directional planning envelopes, not exact daily targets.
